Team Scale‑Consistency in Football Passing Networks (Premier League 2015/16)

Sports analytics · Multiscale adjacency · Correlation

Uncovering Organizational Structures Through Facebook Friendship Networks

Network science · Centrality · Community detection

Structural analysis of a large corporate Facebook network (L2): assortativity, small‑world features, degree distributions, and leadership inference via centralities; communities via modularity optimization.

The Minority Game: Adaptive Competition & Market Dynamics

Agent‑based modeling · Volatility · Phase transition

Simulates minority‑rule markets; reveals regimes controlled by α = 2^M/N, optimal efficiency near α_c, and effects of producers vs. speculators on predictability and gains.
