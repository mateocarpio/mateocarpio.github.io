About

I am a researcher in complex systems and data science. I study how social interactions mediated by networks influence language use and the emergence of non-standard linguistic features. I am interested in quantitative methods, modeling, and efficient algorithms.

Research

Language and online communities

Homophily, adaptation, spatial segregation

Interaction networks (Twitter/X), language assignment, homophily measures, and analysis of non-standard features.

Models on networks

Opinion dynamics, evolutionary games

Voter/AXELROD, Moran processes, structure–state coevolution, and null models.
